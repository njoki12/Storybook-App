⚔️ Chapter 3 — The Knight’s Triumph

🐉
🔥

The dragon lowered its head and spoke: “Bravery is not fighting, but standing true.”

Instead of a battle, the dragon shared its treasure: a glowing gem that symbolized wisdom and courage.

The knight returned to his village, not with gold, but with a gift greater than any riches.

From that day on, people said: “True strength lies in the heart, not the sword.”
