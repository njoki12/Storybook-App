⚔️ Chapter 1 — The Castle Gate

🐉
🔥

Once upon a time, a young knight stood before the gates of a towering castle.

Inside, a fearsome dragon had been guarding a treasure that no one dared to touch.

The knight tightened his armor, raised his sword, and said, “I may be small, but my courage is mighty.”

The gates creaked open, and the adventure began.
