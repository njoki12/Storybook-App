⚔️ Chapter 2 — The Dragon’s Lair

🐉
🔥

The knight walked carefully through the dark halls of the castle.

At the very end, he heard a deep growl. The dragon awoke, its fiery eyes glowing.

The knight bowed respectfully. “Great Dragon, I do not seek to steal. I seek to prove my bravery.”

The dragon roared so loudly the walls shook. Flames lit up the hall. But instead of running, the knight stood tall.

The dragon tilted its head in surprise. No one had ever faced it without fear.
